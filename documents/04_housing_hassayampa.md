# Hassayampa Academic Village (HAV), Tempe Campus

## Who lives here
Hassayampa Academic Village houses students from the W. P. Carey School of
Business. It runs a Business Community Advantage living-learning community that
connects students through shared coursework, study groups, and peer support.

## Rooms and suites
HAV offers double-occupancy accommodations with suite-style bathrooms. Room
options include shared rooms with shared baths (3-bed and standard
configurations) and private rooms with private baths. Twin XL beds (36x80
inches) are standard, with lofted beds available throughout most buildings —
except Building B floors 1–4, which have permanently lofted beds.

## Amenities
- 24-hour campus security and front desk service
- On-site tutoring center and academic advising
- Laundry facilities
- Wireless internet and study lounges
- Hassayampa dining hall (residents are required to have a meal plan)
- P.O.D. convenience market
- Weekly social activities
- Secure key-card access
- Access to recreational facilities
- Modified rooms for students with disabilities

## Location
HAV sits on the south side of the Tempe campus near the Sun Devil Fitness
Complex, the intramural fields, Barrett (the Honors College), and the W. P.
Carey School of Business. Address: 1201 S. McAllister Ave., Tempe, AZ 85281.

## Nearby honors and engineering housing
Barrett, the Honors College Residential Complex, is composed of nine buildings
designed for honors students at all stages, with study spaces, a rooftop garden,
an amphitheatre, and a fine dining hall. Tooker House is one of the residential
communities for Fulton Schools (engineering) students. All of these communities
are clustered on the south side of campus near recreation facilities.
