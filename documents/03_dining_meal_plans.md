# ASU Sun Devil Hospitality — Meal Plans FAQ

## Who is required to have a plan
First-year students on the Tempe, Downtown Phoenix, West, and Polytechnic
campuses must purchase a meal plan each semester. Upper-division and graduate
students in traditional residence halls are also required to have a plan.
Upperclassmen in university apartments (Adelphi Commons II, Cholla, Vista del
Sol, University Towers, Falcon, Mustang, Phantom, Las Casas) are NOT required,
but a plan is strongly recommended.

## Plan types
Traditional plans (Unlimited, Sparky's Favorite Weekly 14), Commuter plans,
Faculty/Staff plans, an All-M&G plan (Maroon & Gold dollars only), and Meal
Exchange options.

## Meal swipes and door rates
Meal swipes are stored on your ASU ID (SunCard) and grant access to 8
Residential Dining Halls across the four campuses. Without a plan you pay with
M&G Dollars, cash, credit, or debit. 2025–2026 door rates:
- Breakfast: $10.00
- Lunch: $12.50
- Dinner: $13.75
- Weekend brunch: $12.50
- Military discount: $9.00 per meal period
Tax applies to cash/credit; no tax when paying with M&G Dollars.

## Maroon & Gold (M&G) Dollars
M&G is a dollar-for-dollar, tax-free declining balance on your ASU ID, usable at
all Sun Devil Hospitality locations. Minimum purchase is $25. There are three
buckets: Mandatory M&G (forfeited if unused by end of summer), Voluntary M&G
(rolls until graduation), and Bonus M&G (a reward for larger purchases). Bonus
tiers: $100–$200 = 5%, $201–$300 = 10%, $301–$400 = 15%, $401+ = 20%.

## Guest meals
The Unlimited plan includes 48 guest meals per semester; Sparky's Favorite
includes 32. Guest meals let you pay for friends or family with your plan.

## Meal Exchange and To-Go
Meal Exchange lets you trade a swipe for a complete meal at designated spots
(for example, on Tempe: MU P.O.D., Tooker House P.O.D., Subway @ Hassayampa,
TLC at Tooker). A reusable To-Go box costs $6.00 (one swipe); request To-Go
before swiping, and you can't also eat inside on the same payment.

## Plan changes
Changes are accepted through the first week of fall and spring via the MyASU
Housing Portal with prorated billing. Most purchases are final except upon
official university withdrawal.
