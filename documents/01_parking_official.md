# ASU Tempe Campus Parking (Parking & Transit Services)

Three permit categories are available: automobile, motorcycle, and sustainable
(HOV) options. Permits are purchased through the transportation portal at
asu.aimsparking.com. Daily and hourly parking is also available across campus.
Annual permits are recommended for anyone on campus three or more days a week.
Display the hangtag permit facing outward from the rearview mirror; an invisible
permit results in citations.

## Commuter automobile permit prices (per year)
- Apache Boulevard, Rural Road, Tyler Street: $950
- Lot 32: $820
- Novus, Fulton Center, Brickyard, Myrtle Avenue, Mill Avenue: $780
- Stadium, Vista del Sol: $720
- Lot 72 (faculty/staff only): $660
- Lot 55, Lot 58, Tempe Centerpoint, Packard Drive: $480
- Lot 59: $280

Lot 59 is the least expensive student commuter option. Garages such as Apache
Boulevard, Rural Road, and Stadium are covered (shaded) but cost more.

## Resident automobile permits
Pricing varies by residential location and garage-floor restrictions (residents
are typically assigned upper floors or rooftops). Sample resident prices:
- All residents / Packard Drive rooftop: $855
- Novus fifth floor and above: $855
- 922 Place / Adelphi Commons / Rural Road third floor and above: $790
- Barrett Complex / Greek Leadership / Hassayampa / Lot 72: $720

## Motorcycle permits
Any moped, motorcycle, or scooter with a VIN that is licensed with the DMV must
display a valid ASU motorcycle permit. Two types:
- Surface lot only (Lots 3, 15, 40, 45, 50, 55, 58, 59, 65, 72): $280
- Garage + surface (Apache Boulevard, Fulton Center, Rural Road, Stadium,
  Myrtle Avenue, University Towers): $480

## Sustainable / HOV (carpool) permits
HOV permits let a rideshare group split the cost of one permit and park in
specially marked spaces, usually on the ground level of a garage or near the
facility entrance. HOV permits are NOT available to on-campus resident students,
and carpool members cannot also hold individual permits. Available at Apache
Boulevard, Packard Drive South, Rural Road, Stadium, Myrtle Avenue, Tyler
Street, Lot 3, Lot 59, and Lot 72.

## Other rules
Sharing a permit to dodge hourly rates may result in a citation, facility fees,
or loss of parking privileges. Prorated permits are available starting
September 5 with monthly pricing that declines through July. Contact PTS at
480-965-6124.
