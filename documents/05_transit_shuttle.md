# ASU Public Transit and Shuttles

## Valley Metro bus and light rail
Valley Metro runs bus and light rail throughout the Phoenix metro area, with at
least one bus stop at each of the four ASU campuses. Light rail stops serve the
Downtown Phoenix and Tempe campuses and connect to Phoenix Sky Harbor
International Airport. Light rail travel between the Downtown Phoenix and Tempe
campuses takes about 35 minutes.

## Tempe FLASH shuttle (free, on-campus)
The Tempe FLASH is a free campus shuttle with two routes connecting campus
buildings and parking. Shuttles run every 12 to 15 minutes, Monday through
Friday from 7 a.m. to 10 p.m. During university holidays and class breaks,
service is limited (7 a.m. to 6 p.m.).

## Intercampus shuttle
ASU runs free shuttle service between the four Phoenix-area campuses (Tempe,
Downtown, West, Polytechnic); your SunCard student ID gets you on for free. The
Polytechnic–Tempe intercampus shuttle is the Gold Route. Shuttles have free
Wi-Fi and charging stations.

## Transit passes (U-Pass)
Discounted transit passes are available to students, faculty, and staff for
Valley Metro buses and light rail, purchased through the ASU parking portal. The
student U-Pass costs $150 (plus a $310 ASU subsidy, for a $460 value) and is
good for unlimited rides on Valley Metro light rail and all Local, Express, and
RAPID bus routes, valid Aug. 16 to May 15.

## Guaranteed Ride Home
HOV permit holders receive two free Uber rides per academic year (up to $30 per
ride) for qualifying situations. Request a ride by calling PTS Dispatch at
480-965-0936, Monday–Friday, 7 a.m. to 7 p.m. PTS also offers a free emergency
ride-home program: stop by any PTS office (8 a.m.–4:30 p.m.) to pick up a free
Valley Metro one-day pass good for bus and light rail.
