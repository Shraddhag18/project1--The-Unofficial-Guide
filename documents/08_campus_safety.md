# Staying Safe on the Tempe Campus

## ASU LiveSafe app
The ASU LiveSafe app is free for students, parents, faculty, and staff and
delivers campus safety advisories, crime alerts, and emergency information.
Download it from the App Store or Google Play and create a profile with your ASU
email or mobile number. You can also use it to request a safety escort.

## Safety Escort service
ASU's Undergraduate Student Government runs the Safety Escort service. Open the
LiveSafe app to request an escort vehicle to one of many key locations around
the Tempe campus; if the app is unavailable, call 480-965-1515. Escort vehicles
run seven days a week from 7 p.m. to 2 a.m. To get a ride, share your student
ID, the number of people, and your location.

## Blue-light emergency call boxes
Blue-light emergency call boxes are placed throughout campus and its parking
lots. They let you contact emergency services — including the ASU Police
Department — without using your phone.

## ASU Police Department contacts
- Emergency: 911
- Non-emergency: 480-965-3456

## Personal safety habits
Stay aware of your surroundings, travel with others at night when possible, keep
your phone charged, and use the escort service rather than walking alone across
campus late at night.
